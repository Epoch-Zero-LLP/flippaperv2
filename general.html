General Information

Who We Are

Flipaper, situated at the foothills of Himalayas in Lalkuan, Uttarkhand, is a division of Flipaper Textiles and Industries Limited. It is the largest manufacturer of Paper, Board, Tissue and Pulp from single location in India with a capacity of 1450 MT per day. Its in operation since 1984 and has been a major player in the domestic and the export market for this industry. The company has service centres and sales offices across the country as well as agents across the world.

Flipaper is one of the most responsible manufacturers in the country taking complete care of environment as well as community. Flipaper is proud to be associated with Aditya Birla Group.
