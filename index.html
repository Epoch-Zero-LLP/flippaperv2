WHO WE ARE
We are situated at the foothills of the Himalayas in Lalkuan, Uttarkhand, is a division of Flipaper Textiles and Industries Limited. It is the largest manufacturer of Paper, Board, Tissue, and Pulp from a single location in India with a capacity of 1450 MT per day. It's in operation since 1984 and has been a major player in the domestic and the export market for this industry. The company has service centers and sales offices across the country as well as agents across the world.
